The Opportunity

Cannabis shoppers are overwhelmed

87% of dispensary customers want guidance but only 23% get it online. The average Dutchie menu has 200+ products. An AI-powered concierge can bridge this gap — bringing budtender expertise to every digital transaction.

1

200+ SKUs

Average dispensary menu size creates decision paralysis for new and experienced shoppers alike.

2

Complex Products

Strain types, terpene profiles, THC/CBD ratios, consumption methods — too many variables for a simple filter.

3

No Personalization

Every customer sees the same menu. No learning from preferences, no adaptive recommendations.

4

Lost Revenue

Cart abandonment is 68% — shoppers leave when they can't decide. Guided selling increases AOV by 35%.

Recommendation

The Recommended Path

We recommend a phased approach combining Strategies 1, 5, and 7 — building from conversational foundation to occasion-based discovery to deep personalization.

Phase 1
💬

Bottom Sheet Budtender

Foundation layer. Always-available AI assistant that learns from every interaction. Lowest effort, highest immediate impact.

Q2 2026 4-6 weeks
→
Phase 2
🎥

Vibe Match

Occasion-based discovery layer. Builds on Phase 1 data to offer curated kits. Drives AOV through bundle selling.

Q3 2026 6-8 weeks
→
Phase 3
🌟

Cannabis Sommelier

Deep personalization. Builds palate profiles from Phase 1+2 data. The ultimate competitive moat — Netflix for cannabis.

Q4 2026 8-10 weeks
Why This Combination
Conversation → Occasion → Personalization
Phase 1 captures intent data from thousands of conversations. Phase 2 uses that data to build occasion models. Phase 3 creates individual palate profiles that get smarter with every purchase. Each phase feeds the next — creating a flywheel that competitors can't replicate.
